About MajesticWicket

MajesticWicket is a global cricket hub built for fans everywhere — from the streets of Mumbai and Lahore to living rooms in London, Sydney, Bridgetown, and Columbus, Ohio.

We cover every major league on the planet: IPL, BBL, PSL, CPL, The Hundred, SA20, MLC, and beyond. We follow every Test series, every T20 World Cup, every Ashes. If it's cricket, it's here — refreshed four times every day.

Our content pipeline pulls from the world's best cricket sources and uses AI curation to surface the stories that matter most. Stats are pulled fresh every day so every league page shows real, current standings and performance leaders.

Why "MajesticWicket"?

A wicket is the set of three stumps and two bails that stands at each end of the pitch. Knocking one down is the bowler's ultimate prize. It's the moment that changes everything. And yes — cricket is majestic.

The US Cricket Opportunity

Cricket is the fastest-growing sport in the United States. Major League Cricket launched in 2023. The ICC T20 World Cup came to American stadiums in 2024. MajesticWicket is here to be the home for global fans and introduce the game to curious American fans discovering it for the first time.
