About MajesticWicket

MajesticWicket is a global cricket hub built for fans everywhere — from the streets of Mumbai and Lahore to living rooms in London, Sydney, Bridgetown, and Columbus, Ohio.

We cover every major league on the planet: IPL, BBL, PSL, CPL, The Hundred, SA20, MLC, and beyond. We follow every Test series, every T20 World Cup, every Ashes. If it's cricket, it's here — refreshed four times every day.

Our content pipeline pulls from the world's best cricket sources and uses AI curation to surface the stories that matter most. Standings and player stats are calculated from real match scorecard data and updated automatically every day.

Why "MajesticWicket"?

A wicket is the set of three stumps and two bails that stands at each end of the pitch. Knocking one down is the bowler's ultimate prize. It's the moment that changes everything. And yes — cricket is majestic.

Cricket in America

Cricket is the fastest-growing sport in the United States. Major League Cricket launched in 2023 with $150M+ in investment. The T20 World Cup came to American stadiums in 2024. Cricket returns to the Olympics at Los Angeles 2028. MajesticWicket is here to be the home for global fans and the gateway for curious Americans discovering the game for the first time.
